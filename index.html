What is it?

Simple Downloader is a user-friendly app for effortlessly downloading YouTube videos and audio. Search or input a link, choose your preferred quality, and enjoy your favorite content offline. Try Simple Downloader today!

How to Use

1

Enter YouTube URL or Search Term

Paste a YouTube video URL in the input field. If you don't have a URL, you can enter a search term and the app will open YouTube search results in your browser.

2

Choose Format and Quality

Select whether you want to download the video or audio only, and choose your preferred quality (Highest, Medium, or Lowest).

3

Select Save Location

Click the "Browse" button to choose where you want to save the downloaded file on your computer.

4

Optional: Set Custom Filename

If you want to save the file with a specific name, enter it in the "Custom Filename" field. Leave it empty to use the original video title.

5

Start Download

Click the "Download" button to start the download process. The progress bar will show the download status, and you'll be notified when it's complete.

Pro Tips:

  • Video Quality: Higher quality videos take longer to download and require more storage space.
  • Audio Only: Select "Audio Only" to extract just the audio track, perfect for music videos.
  • Custom Filename: Use the custom filename feature to organize your downloads better.
  • Search Function: If you don't have the exact URL, use the search function to find videos.
